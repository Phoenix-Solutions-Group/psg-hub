# psg-hub

## What This Is

Unified PSG platform consolidating three product surfaces in one Next.js app, separated by RBAC + tier + security profiles + RLS: (1) customer-facing portal for collision repair shops in the PSG roster (~842 shops) ‚Äî marketing analytics, AI agents, invoicing, payments, post-repair sentiment, market intel; (2) PSG internal operational backbone ‚Äî Companies, Employees, Repair Customers, ROs, Estimates, Surveys, Production printing (PSG's core revenue mail program), 26 operational reports, master data, Security Profiles, RO/Estimate Import; (3) PSG-internal agentic market intelligence ‚Äî competitor engine, Yext (Growth+ tier), weather correlation, multi-LLM router, NotebookLM grounding, agentic report synthesis + PDF.

## Core Value

Consolidates fragmented PSG tooling (BSM customer dashboard, ads-dashboard plans, psg-advantage-portal market intel, psg-import, apps/ads/ mutation tooling, FleetComplete 2019 spec, FileMaker Advantage daily driver) into one branded `hub.psgweb.me` surface that customers, internal staff, and superadmins all use ‚Äî replacing logins and tooling sprawl with role-gated unified access.

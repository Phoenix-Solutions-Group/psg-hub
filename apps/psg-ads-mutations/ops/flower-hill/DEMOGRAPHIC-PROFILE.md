## Competitive Context

### Why This Market is Ideal for Flower Hill
1. **Highest luxury vehicle density on Long Island** — North Shore ZIPs over-index heavily for Porsche, BMW, Mercedes, Land Rover, Aston Martin ownership
2. **Insurance-insulated buyers** — high-value vehicles = higher insurance coverage = less price resistance on repairs
3. **"Only certified" advantage** — Long Island has NO other Aston Martin certified body shop. This is a monopoly for that segment.
4. **EV explosion** — Tesla, Rivian, Lucid owners cannot go to just any shop. Flower Hill's EV certifications are a second monopoly opportunity.

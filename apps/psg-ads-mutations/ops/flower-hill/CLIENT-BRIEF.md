## The Campaign Strategy

We're organizing the campaign into four tiers, each targeting a distinct segment and revenue opportunity.

### Tier 1 — General Collision ("Near Me" Searches)
**Volume:** 60,000–90,000 searches per month nationally  
**CPC:** $2.88–$3.32  
**What:** People who've just had an accident and are searching for the nearest qualified shop  
**How:** Google Search ads with 12–15 mile radius targeting around each location. All three locations run simultaneously. LSA (Google Guaranteed) overlays for maximum visibility.

### Tier 2 — Huntington Launch Push (Priority)
**Volume:** 210–400 searches/month local  
**CPC:** $1.60–$1.89  
**What:** Huntington-specific searches ("huntington auto body," "huntington collision center") where DePalo ranks mid-page organically and nobody is running paid ads  
**How:** Exact match Google Search ads targeting Huntington ZIP codes. We appear above DePalo's organic listing on every relevant search. $200–300/month is sufficient to own this.

This is critical for Huntington because with 14 Google reviews vs. DePalo's 2,593, the LSA local pack ranking is not yet competitive. Paid search bypasses the review count disadvantage entirely.

### Tier 3 — EV Monopoly (Rivian, Lucid, Tesla)
**Volume:** 390–880 searches/month nationally, growing fast  
**CPC:** $4.45–$16.72  
**What:** EV owners searching for certified collision centers who can actually service their vehicles. No other shop on Long Island is Rivian or Lucid certified. Owners currently drive to NYC or NJ.  
**How:** Google Search ads for "rivian certified collision center," "rivian body shop," "lucid collision repair," and related EV terms. Zero paid competition means these ads run at the floor price.

**Trend note:** "Electric vehicle collision repair" search volume has risen from a trend score of 0.33 to 1.00 over the past 12 months — demand is doubling as EV adoption accelerates across Long Island's 3,033 registered EVs in the target ZIPs.

### Tier 4 — Luxury Brand Certifications (Aston Martin, Ferrari, Porsche, Audi)
**Volume:** 90–590 searches/month per make  
**CPC:** $2.75–$59.86  
**What:** Owners of exotic and ultra-luxury vehicles searching specifically for shops certified by their manufacturer  
**How:** Exact match Google Search for brand-specific terms. No paid competitors. Flower Hill is Long Island's only certified Aston Martin repair center — the $59.86 CPC is irrelevant against the value of one conversion.

## Parallel: Review Generation (Must Run Alongside Ads)

Huntington's 14 reviews are the single biggest vulnerability in this plan. Google LSA heavily weights review count. This is the fix:

- SMS review request sent at every vehicle pickup at Huntington
- Email follow-up 3 days after pickup
- **Target: 50 Google reviews at Huntington by Month 3**
- **Target: 100+ reviews by Month 6**

With 50 reviews, Huntington becomes competitive in the LSA local pack. With 100+, it begins to challenge DePalo's positioning with newer, more recent reviews.

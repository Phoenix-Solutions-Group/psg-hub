## Situation Summary

Flower Hill Auto Body has operated for **75+ years across four generations** on the North Shore of Long Island. Three locations serve the Nassau/Suffolk Gold Coast corridor. They hold **25+ OEM certifications** including the exclusive designation as **Long Island's ONLY certified Aston Martin Repair Center**. They are not currently running paid advertising.

Two challenges define this launch:
1. **Huntington is a brand new location with only 14 Google reviews** — compared to competitors with 600-2,593. Ads must lean on the parent brand's 75-year legacy and OEM credentials, not the new location's review count.
2. **Dual audience tension** — the exotic/luxury positioning must coexist with general collision repair without one diluting the other.

## Strategic North Star

> **Establish Flower Hill as the undisputed premium collision repair authority on North Shore Long Island — leading with the certifications no competitor can match, building Huntington's review base fast, and owning every premium ZIP code in the 4,631-household target market.**

## Audience Segments

### Segment A: Luxury/Exotic Vehicle Owners
- **Demographics:** HHI $200k+, age 38-65, homeowners, executives/business owners
- **Vehicle makes:** Aston Martin, McLaren, Porsche, Bentley, Rolls-Royce, Maserati, Maybach, Ferrari, Lamborghini, BMW M-series, Mercedes AMG
- **Primary ZIPs:** 11568, 11560, 11765, 11030, 11791, 11050
- **Key insight:** The Aston Martin certification is a monopoly. No other shop on Long Island can claim it. This is a "category of one" position.
- **Where to reach:** Google Search (brand-specific keywords), Instagram, YouTube pre-roll on auto content

### Segment B: Premium Daily Driver Owners
- **Demographics:** HHI $100k-$200k, age 30-60
- **Vehicle makes:** BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Lexus, Tesla, Rivian, Land Rover, Volvo, Genesis, Acura (all in NSCHOOLCRAFT-143 list)
- **Primary ZIPs:** Tier 1 (New Hyde Park, Syosset, Port Washington, Roslyn, Manhasset)
- **Key insight:** 4,631 households from the database = a defined, reachable market. These owners won't go to just any shop with their $60-100k vehicle.
- **Where to reach:** Google Search, Meta, Google LSA

### Segment C: EV Vehicle Owners — Long Island Monopoly

**Critical insight from PSG intelligence report: No Rivian or Lucid certified collision centers exist anywhere on Long Island. Owners currently must go to NYC or New Jersey.**

This is not an emerging opportunity — it is a monopoly waiting to be claimed through advertising.
- **Makes:** Tesla, Rivian, Lucid, Polestar — plus BMW iX/I4/I7, Porsche Taycan, Mercedes EQS/EQE, Audi e-tron (all OEM-certified at Flower Hill)
- **Profile:** Tech-forward, age 30-55, high income, conscientious buyers
- **Market size:** 3,033 registered EVs across 29 target ZIPs (LocalReach data). Top EV ZIPs: Syosset (188), Huntington Station (184), New Hyde Park (167), Port Washington (143).
- **Highest-value EV targets:** Rivian R1S (Flower Hill has Fleet cert — very rare), Porsche Taycan, BMW iX/I7, Mercedes EQS
- **Key insight:** Most shops can't touch EVs properly. Flower Hill's Tesla + Rivian Fleet certifications = another defensible moat. Huntington Station has the highest EV brand diversity (38 makes) in the market.
- **Where to reach:** Google Search (EV-specific terms), Meta custom audience, Rivian/Tesla owner communities

## Budget Pacing Philosophy

### 70 / 20 / 10 Framework
| Tier | Allocation | What |
|------|-----------|------|
| Proven (70%) | $4,200 | Google Search + LSA (direct response, measurable) |
| Scaling (20%) | $1,200 | Meta prospecting (brand + upper funnel) |
| Testing (10%) | $600 | Microsoft/Bing, YouTube pre-roll (Month 3+), new creative tests |

### Phase Pacing
| Phase | Months | Budget Behavior |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| Launch & Learn | 1-2 | Full budget, conservative bids, Maximize Clicks, track everything |
| Optimize | 3-4 | Shift to Maximize Conversions once 15+ conversions/campaign/month |
| Scale | 5-6 | Target CPA bidding on winners, kill losers, test YouTube |
| Maintain | 7+ | 70/20/10 steady state with quarterly reallocation reviews |

## Budget Review Triggers

Increase budget when:
- CPL drops below $60 (scale that platform 20%)
- Calls exceed capacity (coordinate with client before scaling)
- Exotic inquiry volume hits 10+/month (unlock YouTube budget)

Decrease/pause when:
- CPL exceeds $200 for 2 consecutive weeks without improving
- Call quality score (booked job rate) drops below 20%
- Platform CTR below 2% for 30 days (creative refresh needed first)

## Scaling Rules

### 20% Scale Rule
- CPL < $75 for 7+ consecutive days with ≥10 conversions/week → increase budget 20%
- Wait 5 days before next increase
- Never increase Meta by >20% in 7-day window (triggers learning reset)

### 3× Kill Rule
- Any ad group spends >$225 (3× $75 CPL) with zero qualified leads → pause
- Review: keywords, landing page, bid strategy, tracking
- Don't restart without changes

### Toyota-Specific Kill Rule
- Toyota campaign spends >$400/month with <3 qualified leads → audit keyword intent
- Check search term report for model-specific traffic with no certification intent
- Add offending queries to negative list before resuming

### LSA Budget Management
- CPL >$75 for 2 consecutive weeks → reduce max per-lead bid $5
- CPL <$40 for 2 consecutive weeks → increase max per-lead bid $5

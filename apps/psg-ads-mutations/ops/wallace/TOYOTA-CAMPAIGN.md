## Competitive Intelligence

### Primary Threat: Toyota of Kingsport Collision Center

Their claim: "The only Toyota and Lexus Certified Collision Center within 100 miles"

This is the primary ad blocker for Wallace. Toyota of Kingsport is actively marketing exclusivity. Wallace must directly counter this.

**Their weaknesses:**
- Dealer-owned — customers assume dealer markup and upsell pressure
- Attached to a sales floor — intimidating for collision customers
- Likely lower ad aggressiveness (captive service department referrals reduce urgency)
- Not all-insurers independent

**Wallace counter-positioning:**
- Independent certified shop — no dealer markup
- All major insurers accepted
- Multi-brand certified (Toyota + Tesla + JLR) = specialist credibility
- Faster turnaround (no dealership service queue competing for bays)

# Wallace Collision Center — Paid Advertising Strategy
**Client:** Wallace Collision Center
**Agency:** Phoenix Solutions Group
**Market:** Tri-Cities Metro (Kingsport, Johnson City, Bristol, TN)
**Budget Range:** $4,000–$10,000/month
**Goal:** Qualified collision repair leads (form + phone ≥60s)
**Status:** Rebuild — existing account, broken foundation
**Date:** 2026-05-06 (updated with SEMRush live data)

---

## Executive Summary

Wallace Collision is spending ~$4,040/month in Google Ads generating zero reliably measurable leads because conversion tracking counts page loads as conversions. The local search campaign shows genuine buying intent. Tesla and JLR certified-brand campaigns burn 40–55% of budget on research and navigation traffic with no collision intent.

**The rebuild-then-scale model:**
1. Fix tracking — every optimization downstream depends on a clean signal
2. Launch Toyota Certified campaign — highest-volume certified opportunity in market
3. Rebuild campaign structure — tighten certified-brand campaigns, rescue core local
4. Add Google LSA — lowest CPL for local service ($25–75/lead vs. $85–90 Search avg)
5. Add Meta — retargeting, local awareness, Toyota/luxury car owner audiences
6. Scale — once CPA proven, expand toward $10K/month

Same $4,040/month, post-rebuild: projected 40–70 real leads/month at $55–85 CPL.
Full $10K/month: projected 100–150 real leads/month.

## Business Context

### Service Area
- **Primary:** Kingsport, TN and 40-mile radius (Tri-Cities metro)
- **Certified demand:** Tesla, Toyota, JLR — draws from wider 75-mile radius
- **Metro population:** ~500,000 (Tri-Cities combined)

### Competitive Position
- Toyota Collision Care Certified (priority — highest volume certified brand)
- Tesla Approved Collision Repair (rare; within 75 miles)
- Jaguar/Land Rover Certified Collision (rare; within 75 miles)
- I-CAR Gold Class
- All major insurance carriers accepted
- Lifetime warranty on repairs

### Toyota Certified — The Primary Differentiator

Toyota is the best-selling vehicle brand in the US. Camry, RAV4, Tacoma, Tundra, Highlander, 4Runner represent massive local volume. No independent Toyota certified competitor exists in the Tri-Cities market — only Toyota of Kingsport (dealer) and Toyota of Bristol (dealer) hold comparable certification.

Wallace's positioning: **"Certified repair without the dealer experience."**

This is the wedge that separates Wallace from:
- Dealers (markup perception, all-manufacturer bias)
- National franchises (no OEM certification)
- Local independents (no OEM certification)

Certified campaign priority:
1. **Toyota Collision Care** — highest volume, broadest audience, largest revenue opportunity
2. **Tesla Approved** — rare certification, high RO value
3. **JLR Certified** — rare certification, affluent demographic

## Competitive Landscape

### Key Competitors

- **Caliber Collision** — national franchise, high ad spend, brand recognition. No Toyota/Tesla/JLR OEM cert.
- **Joe Hudson's Collision** — national franchise, aggressive PPC, DRP-focused. No OEM cert.
- **Gerber Collision / Crash Champions** — national franchises, no OEM certification.
- **Toyota of Kingsport Collision** — only Toyota certified dealer. Claims "only shop within 100 miles." Dealer stigma.
- **Local independents** — low ad spend, I-CAR Gold at best, no OEM certification.

### Wallace's Unfair Advantage

National franchises cannot claim Toyota, Tesla, or JLR OEM certification.
Local independents aren't running LSA or certified-brand campaigns.
Wallace's certifications are the moat — currently underutilized in ads.

# Wallace Collision — Creative Brief
**Date:** 2026-05-06 (updated with Toyota as primary certified campaign)

---

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Trustworthy, expert, local. Not corporate. Not flashy.
**Personality:** The shop that insurance companies recommend and neighbors trust. OEM certifications, real guarantees, no dealer games.
**Key differentiators to communicate:**
1. Toyota Collision Care Certified — primary certified campaign; highest volume opportunity
2. Tesla Approved — rare; competitors cannot claim this
3. JLR Certified — rare; competitors cannot claim this
4. Lifetime warranty on all repairs
5. All major insurance carriers
6. I-CAR Gold Class technicians
7. Local, not a franchise
8. Independent — not a dealer, no dealer markup

**Toyota counter-messaging (primary):** "Certified repair doesn't require a dealership. Wallace Collision is Toyota Certified — no dealer markup, all insurers accepted."

## Meta Ad Creative Plan

### Priority 1 — Before/After (P1, produce first)

**Format:** Single image or carousel (before/after swipe)  
**Specs:** 1080×1080 (feed), 1080×1920 (story/reel)  
**Content:**
- 3–5 real before/after examples (different vehicle types and damage levels)
- Overlay text: "Before Wallace" / "After Wallace"
- Headline: "Kingsport's Trusted Collision Shop"
- CTA: "Get a Free Estimate"

**Quantity needed:** 5 before/after pairs

### Priority 2 — Customer Testimonial Video (P1, produce within 30 days)

**Format:** 15–30 second vertical video (9:16)  
**Script structure:**
- 0–3s: Hook — customer on camera: "I was rear-ended and had no idea what to do"
- 3–15s: Experience: "Wallace handled everything — insurance, rental car, kept me updated"
- 15–25s: Result: show vehicle looking perfect
- 25–30s: Endorsement: "I'd never go anywhere else"
- End card: Wallace logo + phone + "Free Estimates"

**Quantity needed:** 3–5 testimonial videos

### Priority 3 — Shop / Team Credibility (P2)

**Format:** Static image carousel or short video  
**Content:**
- Shop interior / paint booth / frame equipment
- Team photo (technicians in uniform)
- Certification badges on wall (Tesla, JLR, I-CAR Gold)
- Headline: "Behind every repair is a certified team"
- CTA: "Schedule Your Estimate"

**Quantity needed:** 1 carousel (5 images), 1 shop walkthrough video (30–60s)

### Priority 4 — Offer / Urgency (P2)

**Format:** Static image with bold text overlay  
**Content:**
- "Hail Season Is Here — Get Your Damage Assessed Before Repair Costs Rise"
- "Free Estimate. No Appointment Needed."
- "Insurance Accepted. Rental Assistance Available."
- CTA button: "Call Now" or "Get Estimate"

**Quantity needed:** 2–3 seasonal static images

### Priority 4b — Toyota Certified (P1, produce alongside urgency creative)

**Format:** Static image + short video (15s)
**Target:** Toyota vehicle interest audience (Camry, RAV4, Tacoma, Tundra, 4Runner, Highlander) + website visitors 30-day
**Content:**
- Static: Toyota Collision Care badge + "Certified repair. Without the dealer experience."
- Static: Split image — dealer showroom (intimidating) vs. Wallace shop (approachable) — "Both are Toyota Certified. Only one is on your side."
- Video: certification badge reveal → shop footage → "Your Toyota. Fixed right. By the only independent Toyota Certified shop in Tri-Cities."
- CTA: "Get a Free Toyota Estimate"

**Quantity needed:** 2 statics, 1 short video (15s)

### Priority 5 — Certified Brand Ads (P2, Meta remarketing only)

**Format:** Static image
**Target:** Website visitors who viewed certification pages OR Meta audience of Tesla/LR/Jaguar owners
**Content:**
- Tesla: "Your Tesla was engineered to exact tolerances. Only a Tesla Approved shop can restore it right."
- JLR: "Jaguar and Land Rover owners trust Wallace Collision — the region's JLR Certified body shop."
- CTA: "Schedule an Estimate"

**Quantity needed:** 2 images (one Tesla, one JLR)
